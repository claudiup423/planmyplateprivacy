# Privacy Policy for PlanMyPlate

Last updated: January 31, 2026

## Overview

PlanMyPlate is a meal planning and grocery list app. Your privacy is important to us.

## Data Collection

PlanMyPlate does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your meals, grocery lists, and preferences are stored locally on your device only.

## Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display banner ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. You can remove all ads with an in-app purchase. For more information on Google's data practices, visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## In-App Purchases

Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time through Settings > Clear All Data within the app, or by uninstalling the app.

## Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13.
